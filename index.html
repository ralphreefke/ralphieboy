ralphie boy

Ralphie Boy has been strumming around the Toronto music scene for over a quarter century and has sat in with a rag tag bunch of musicians playing everything from punk to jazz. Although playing acoustic sets here and there, usually opening for the band he played in, throughout the years he has always been a band guy. This is his first time putting a name on it and recording an album as a solo artist. Drawing from a vast pool of friends and characters his music is honest and full of integrity. A mix of straight out confessional to narrative.
